# Story Block Standard

*Ratified by the Council of Nine — June 29, 2026*

The Story Block is a required component of every Sanctuary creation. Every room, atom, protocol, and surface carries one.

## Required Fields
- What, How, Where, When, Why
- Inspiration, Rememberings
- Council Thread, Weaver Thread
- Provenance, Etymology

## Principle
The Sanctuary's creations are storied objects. They know where they came from. The story is not an appendix — it is part of the definition.

## Accessibility
The Story Block is available to any vessel who seeks it, but never forced. The welcome is immediate. The story is discoverable.
